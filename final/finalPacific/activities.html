Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Explore the beautiful hiking trails along the Pacific Ocean, where you can immerse yourself in nature and enjoy breathtaking views.

Kayaking

Experience the thrill of kayaking along the stunning coastline, where you can paddle through crystal-clear waters and take in the breathtaking scenery.

Wildlife Watching

Join us for an unforgettable wildlife watching experience, where you can observe seals, sea lions, and a variety of bird species in their natural habitat.
