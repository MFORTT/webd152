The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a Yurt?
A yurt is a portable, circular dwelling traditionally used by nomadic cultures in Central Asia. It consists of a wooden frame covered with felt or fabric, providing a unique and cozy living space.
Why Stay in a Yurt?
Staying in a yurt offers a unique experience that combines the comforts of home with the beauty of nature. Enjoy the tranquility and rustic charm while being surrounded by stunning landscapes.
Yurt Features
Our yurts are equipped with comfortable bedding, electricity, and access to shared bathroom facilities. Each yurt provides a peaceful retreat after a day of exploring the great outdoors.
